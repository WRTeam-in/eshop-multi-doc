## Adding New FAQ

![Add FAQ](../../../static/backend/img/add_faq1.jpg)
<p align="center">Sample image</p>

Steps:
1. In **Question** field, enter the question.
2. In **Answer** field, enter the answer.
3. Click **Add FAQ** to add FAQ or **Close** to close the form.
