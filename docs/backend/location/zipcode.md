## Adding New Zipcode

![Add New Zipcode](../../../static/backend/img/new_zipcode.jpg)
<p align="center">Sample Image</p>

Steps:
1. In **Zipcode** field, enter the name of the Zipcode.
2. Click **Add Zipcode** button to add the Zipcode or **Reset** to reset the form.
